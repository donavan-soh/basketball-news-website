FIBA 3x3 World Tour 2021

By Candidate No. WP1636

Here are the things you can expect from FIBA 3x3 World Tour Doha Masters 2021.


Where to catch Doha Masters 2021

For live updates, you can follow FIBA's Twitter and Insagram.

To watch it live, you can follow FIBA's Facebook and Youtube.


Who will be competing in Doha Masters 2021

FIBA 3x3 competition. (Photo taken from www.fiba3x3.com)


There will be a total of 14 teams participating in this year's FIBA 3x3 competition:

  • Liman
  • Riga
  • Ub
  • NY Harlem
  • Princeton
  • Novi Sad
  • Amsterdam Talent&Pro
  • Piran
  • Sakiai Gulbele
  • Edmonton
  • Lusail
  • Graz
  • Doha
  • Manila Chooks TM

Which teams you should look out for

The two time defending champion Doha should definitely be number one on your watchlist this year as the Latvians look to continue their streak for the third straight World Tour champion. With the newest addition of World Tour 2020 MVP Nauris Miezis to the team, Doha is a very strong contender this year.

Behind Doha is Riga. Having faced Doha the past two years for the championship title and losing it, Riga will definitely be fighting their way back to the top.


Team Riga's FIBA 3x3 highlights and interviews. (Video taken from FIBA 3x3 channel on YouTube)


Ub may well be on their way to win their first championship title with the additions of Dejan Majstorovic and Marko Savic. The former duo from Novi Sad are unstoppable together on court, making the Serbian a tough contender as well.

Not forgetting the teams from America, NY Harlem and Princeton, the two former champions. They will surely put up a great fight to get back the title.


FIBA 3x3 World Tour Doha Masters 2021 will definitely be an exciting one. However due to Covid-19, fans from all around the world may not get to catch it live in person at Doha. The atmosphere will be different from past years' world tour, but it will be a special and memorable one for sure as live audiences will be streamed on screens. Hope you are looking forward to FIBA 3x3 World Tour as much as I am!
